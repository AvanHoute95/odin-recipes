Boiled Water

Description

This recipe will be sure to leave you with some tasty piping hot water that will absolutely scald you!

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Stove
  • Pot

Directions

  1. Fill pot with water to whatever level you want
  2. Crank that burner all the way up and put pot on the burner. Don't touch!
  3. Stare intently at the pot until it starts to boil
  4. Check your watch every 10 seconds
  5. When it gets to a rolling boil, immediately remove water from the pot and place inside a tupperware.
  6. Once all the water is in a tupperware, move it to the freezer. Now when you need boiling water, just take it out and let it thaw. Bone app the Teeth!
